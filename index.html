About Me

Welcome to my homepage. I am a visiting B.S. with SSR Fellowship at Stanford University, focusing on Humanoid Intelligence (HI): Cognitive Learning, World Interaction and Policy Simulation.

My work explores the potential for the digital life of artificial intelligence and application in world factors, represented by quantitative finance.

I am a senior undergraduate at UW-Madison (CDIS, ISci#11, CS#13), also an Amazon Scholar in Trustworthy AI research, previously at Peking University.
